ABOUT US
Top Dog grew out of a boy's love of sausage, a staple in his German immigrants' New York home over the WWII years. His paper route to a well-mixed neighborhood assured that Italian, Polish, even Hungarian sausages were no strangers to that developing palate. As in that boy's world, we continue to seek out the best sausage from the best sausage-makers who tend to serve it better.
